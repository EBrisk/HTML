Magic is the art of illusions and doing impossible things. There are 10 main types of magic, each with its own unique set of techniques and effects. These days magicians combine several types of magic in one magic trick.

By clicking on each of the types you can see videos demonstrating the type of magic and even learn a magic or two.

levitation

Levitation is the act of making an object appear to be defying gravity and floating in mid-air. This is often achieved by using sleight of hand, mirrors, or wires.

vanish

Vanishing is the act of making an object disappear. This is often achieved by using sleight of hand, misdirection, or quick movements.

production

Production is the act of making an object appear out of thin air. This is often achieved by using hidden compartments, sleight of hand, or misdirection.

transformation

Transformation is the act of changing an object from one thing into another. This is often achieved by using clever props, sleight of hand, or misdirection.

transposition

Transposition is the act of switching two objects places. This is often achieved by using sleight of hand, misdirection, or quick movements.

penetration

Penetration is the act of an object pass through a solid object. This is often done with the use of sleight of hand, misdirection, or clever props.

restoration

Restoration is the act of restoring an object to its original state after it has been damaged or destroyed. This is often achieved by using clever props, sleight of hand, or misdirection.

Escape

Escape is the act of freeing oneself from a seemingly impossible situation. This is often achieved by using knowledge of physics, sleight of hand, or misdirection.

teleportation

Teleportation is the act of instantly transporting an object from one place to another. This is often achieved by using sleight of hand, misdirection, or quick movements.

prediction

Prediction is the act of foretelling the future. This is often achieved by using psychology, cold reading, or misdirection.
